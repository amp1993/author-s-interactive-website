Sam Satito

Sam Satito is the story of a puppy who dreams of finding a family of his own. He’s given to a little girl as a birthday present, but she does not appreciate his company and, very soon, he’s left to his own devices. Sam is hungry and cold on the streets, but never loses hope of finding a home where he can give all his love and care to humans, a mission that ́s given to all dogs. Will Sam find the family he wants so much? Join Sam Satito on his journey to find the family who will accept him and fulfill his mission of love.

Sam Satito cuenta la historia de un perrito que sueña con tener una familia propia. Lo entregan como regalo de cumpleaños a una niña que no aprecia su compañía y, muy pronto, lo abandona a su suerte. Sam pasa hambre y mucho frío, pero no pierde la esperanza de encontrar un hogar donde pueda regalar todo su amor y cuidados a los humanos, una misión dada a todos los perros. ¿Logrará Sam conseguir la familia que tanto desea? Acompaña a Sam Satito en su travesía para encontrar su familia y cumplir su misión de amor.
